Building civic engagement tools that work beyond election cycles

Our Mission: Traditional campaigns create intense activity before elections, then fall silent afterward. We're building tools to maintain civic networks year-round, keep candidates accountable, and empower communities to stay engaged between elections.

What We're Building

ACCOUNTABILITY

Campaign Promise Repository

Create a structured repository of campaign promises made by candidates, allowing voters to track commitments over time.

  • Increases candidate accountability
  • Provides voters with concrete evaluation criteria
  • Enables historical comparison across election cycles
ACCOUNTABILITY

AI-Powered Promise Monitoring

Automated systems to scan news sources and identify when campaign promises are fulfilled, broken, or in progress.

  • Real-time accountability tracking
  • Reduces manual research burden
  • Natural language processing for promise extraction
SUSTAINED ENGAGEMENT

Post-Election Network Persistence

Transform campaign tools into ongoing civic engagement platforms that maintain community networks after elections.

  • Breaks the boom-bust cycle of traditional campaigns
  • Builds long-term civic capacity
  • Creates sustained accountability mechanisms
SUSTAINED ENGAGEMENT

Group Leaders as News Channels

Group leaders serve as trusted channels for distributing news and event information to their members.

  • Keeps members informed between election cycles
  • Leverages existing trust relationships
  • Maintains network engagement post-election
PRIVACY & FLEXIBILITY

Privacy-First Design

Optional candidate relationship tracking and flexible group participation.

  • Optional fields to encourage broad participation
  • Single membership, multiple group leadership
  • Privacy considerations built into every feature
EXPONENTIAL REACH

Call-5 Strategy

Each supporter contacts five people who then replicate the process, creating exponential outreach without traditional campaign infrastructure.

  • Personal networks drive engagement
  • Mathematical scaling without mass media
  • Sustained one-to-one voter connections
